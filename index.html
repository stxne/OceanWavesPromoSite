OCEAN WAVES

A TOMOMI MOCHIZUKI FILM

ABOUT

Ocean Waves, known in Japan as I Can Hear the Sea, is a 1993 Japanese anime television film directed by Tomomi Mochizuki and written by Kaori Nakamura based on the 1990-1992 novel of the same name by Saeko Himuro. It was animated by Studio Ghibli for Tokuma Shoten and the Nippon Television Network. Ocean Waves first aired on May 5, 1993 on Nippon TV. It concerns a love triangle that develops between two good friends and a new girl who transfers to their high school from Tokyo.
